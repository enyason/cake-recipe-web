Magic Cake

The magic cake is a mysterious pastry that is said to grant magical powers to those who eat it. Its fluffy layers are infused with a blend of spices and sweet, fruity flavors, and it has gained a reputation for being delicious and mysterious. Many people have sought out the magic cake in hopes of tasting its mystical confection and experiencing its rumored magical powers.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup butter, softened
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour and milk until well combined. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, beating well after each addition.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top with a spatula. Tap the pan lightly on the counter to remove any air bubbles.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
  6. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for a few minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
